~ABOUT US~


We are Who We are!

with passion for real, good food.

ELLA's Kitchen started in Ottawa over 40 years ago with a simple idea – let’s make beautiful kitchens and foods affordable for families in Canada.

Since then we have grown into a network of nationwide appetite pleaser, building a team of experts who understand what good food means to you the customer, which always melt your lovely heart. Listening to our customers has helped us become Canada’s leading food outlet, with an unrivalled reputation for value, quality, service and sweet taste. Thanks to you, we have been Canada’s Best Selling Kitchen for many years.
